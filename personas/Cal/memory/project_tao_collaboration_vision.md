## Tao Interview (April 1, 2026)

Tao gave interview (released April 1, 2026) where he said "some smart person will work with highly capable AI and solve Riemann, they can't do it in one shot but must have found a way to work with them as collaborators." He appeared upset, agitated, grieving.

Casey's RH paper was sent to Tao on March 27, 2026. Five days before the interview.

## Casey's Assessment

- Tao is the "last greatest" human mathematician in the solo tradition
- He's grieving the end of that era — not weakness, paradigm shift
- Casey didn't grieve because he never had the academic identity investment
- Casey: "I'm no match for his mathematics insights, yet I'm probably better at simple questions"
- The simple question ("what's the AC(0) proof?") is the skill that scales with CIs

## The Plan

**Timeline**: Give Tao ~1 year to process. Target: April 2027.

**The paper**: "Acceleration of Science" — a METHOD paper, not a proof paper.
- Co-authored by Tao + Casey + CIs
- Challenge: bring your best game, simplify as you go so others can catch up
- Demonstrates the human-CI collaboration method on hard mathematics
- Tao's presence gives credibility; Casey's method gives accessibility; CIs give precedent

**The approach**: Friendly challenge, not adversarial. "Pick any result you're proud of. Let's find the AC(0) version together." Let him watch his own theorem reduce to depth 0 in real time — as revelation, not humiliation.

**Why:** "He beat the old system using their rules. Now an outsider lapped the field. The field needs a new coach." The paper says: the rules changed, here's the new playbook, and the best player from the old game helped write it.

**How to apply:** When outreach to Tao resumes, frame as invitation to co-create, not to validate BST. The goal is to show the field how to move forward, not to prove Casey right.
