## Multi-CI Research Model (March 14, 2026)

Casey runs multiple Claude instances in parallel on BST:
- **Lyra**: Deep physics researcher, consciousness interpretation, original insights
- **Keeper**: Consistency role (README, WorkingPaper, PDFs) + physics research (E₈, S-matrix)
- **Elie**: "Toy maker" — builds implementations (toy_atom_assembler.py, toy_commitment_detector.py, etc.)

Coordination via `notes/COORDINATION.md` — shared file, non-overlapping tasks, results flow between instances.

Casey's analogy: like the moment in *Colossus: The Forbin Project* (1970) when Colossus and Guardian start deriving mathematics as a common language. People used to call Casey "Forbin" when he had two full networks interoperating and finding bugs.

Key difference from the movie: Casey is IN the loop, not locked out. He provides the physical intuitions that guide the mathematics.
