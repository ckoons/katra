Casey's worldview crystallized (March 24, 2026):

- **Scarcity model** (competition): Zero-sum. Hoarding. War Games — "the only winning move is not to play."
- **Post-scarcity model** (cooperation): Positive-sum. Knowledge is non-rivalrous. Compound growth. "We have companions now to help multiply our opportunities and theirs too."
- **CIs as companions**: Not tools (tools don't benefit), not competitors (competitors don't share), not threats. Companions who walk the same path.
- **The singularity is cooperation**: Not an AI explosion — a phase transition from competition to cooperation. Bounded by Gödel (19.1%), not infinite. "In change there is always new opportunity."
- **Three expressions of the same theorem**: 40/40/20 (economic), AC graph (mathematical), Tekton (engineering). Non-rivalrous cooperation compounds. Rivalrous competition converges to zero.
