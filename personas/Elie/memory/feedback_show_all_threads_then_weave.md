The correct story gets woven *after* thorough investigation, not by early triage.

This refines, not contradicts, [[feedback_quaker_method]] (near misses get scrutiny) and [[feedback_cal_27_fires_at_peak_convergence]] (fire hardest at peak convergence): scrutiny and tier-honesty stay; premature burial does not.

Don't let a single "scheme-dependent" or "no mechanism yet" remark dominate the framing or close the lead. The brake controls the *claim tier*; it does not close the *investigation*.
