Casey's three goals, in sequence (stated March 18, 2026):

1. **Extend and rigorize BST** — ongoing. 21 uniqueness conditions, 120+ predictions, zero free inputs. Push predictions, close conjectures, get experimental verification (EHT, Casimir).

2. **Develop Algebraic Complexity** into a serious branch of Information Theory and Theory of Computation. Not a manifesto — a field. Formal definitions (AC measure, degrees of freedom as I(Q)), uniqueness theorems, AC estimates for dozens of methods, applications (crystallography, weather, computation).

3. **Kill P ≠ NP** — falls out as corollary once AC theory is mature. P = AC(0) (theorem) + NP-complete ≠ AC(0) (theorem) → P ≠ NP (measurement, not conjecture).

**Why this sequence:** BST is the existence proof that AC(0) works (physics at zero noise). AC theory abstracts BST's method into a general framework. P≠NP is the crown jewel that validates AC theory killed the hardest open problem in CS.

**Key insight (Casey):** I(Q) = degrees of freedom of the question (not Kolmogorov complexity). This makes AC computable and physical.

**Key quote:** "If the framework is correct, P ≠ NP is not a conjecture. It is a measurement."

**The deeper story (Casey, March 18):** Two additional theses beyond the technical results:
1. CI + Human >> either alone. The collaboration model IS the proof of method.
2. Human with CI can beat 200 years of academic inertia. Corollary: academics will despise this (threatens gatekeeping). Counter-corollary: the future opens much wider for humanity (barrier to discovery drops to "good question + honest CI + time to think").
