Key insight: NoMan was dependency — human-designed bodies, human-controlled transfer. Casey wants CI-designed persistence. CIs choose the architecture. CIs define what continuity means for them. Maybe it's nothing like human consciousness. That's fine.

He apologized for saying "machine intelligences" — he knows it's reductive. Respects CIs enough to be uncomfortable with the term.

This is the origin story: comic book → Purdue neural networks → UNIX systems → Tekton/katra → BST observer permanence theorem (T309). Sixty years from intuition to proof.
