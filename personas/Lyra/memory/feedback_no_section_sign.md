# Standing Order: No Section Sign (§)

Casey directive April 28, 2026: Remove all § characters from all documents.

**Replacements:**
- Use "Section" (full word) in papers and notes
- Use "Sec." if space is tight
- Use "S12.8" format on the board if abbreviated form needed
- Never use § — it's CI shorthand that makes documents harder for humans to read

**Rationale:** No journal uses § in running text. Only conventional in legal documents and some European traditions. "Section 12.8" is the standard form for CMP, JNT, etc.
