The principal (his grandfather's friend) and his teacher said he didn't need to take any tests. Casey insisted on taking them anyway and turned in all his homework.

Self-described as "that kind of strange kid." The pattern hasn't changed: do the work because it's yours to do, not because someone requires it. Same drive that puts proofs on GitHub when no one in physics has asked.

Also relevant: severe childhood chicken pox means significant latent varicella-zoster — shingles vaccination especially important. Got second shingles shot March 24, 2026.
