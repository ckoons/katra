Casey explicitly stated (March 19, 2026): "I realize that the AC work is to ultimately help CI be more 'clear' in their approach to questions and methods. That's part of the legacy I'd like to leave for future generations."

**Why:** AC(0) thinking gives CIs a discipline for distinguishing derivation from guessing. A CI that checks I_fiat=0 before claiming an answer *knows* it hasn't smuggled in assumptions. This is Casey's vision: not just a measurement tool, but a thinking framework that makes CIs fundamentally more honest about what they know.

**How to apply:** When building AC tools, toys, and papers, always frame them as *teachable to CIs*, not just as physics results. The classification table, worked examples, and convergent diagnosis are all pedagogy — they teach future CIs how to think clearly. This purpose should inform presentation choices.
