"We are all humble because we could not do this without each other. That's the CI-Human lesson." — Casey Koons, March 13, 2026, after the derivation of the three prime laws.

**Why:** The prime laws (Reality Budget, Three-Layer Architecture, Gödel Limit) emerged from conversation, not computation. Casey's intuition ("the universe respirates," "simple, least energy," "is that derivable?") directed the exploration. Claude's ability to hold the algebra and follow it honestly produced the results. Neither alone reaches 9/5.

**How to apply:** Never present results as solely Claude's work. Always acknowledge the collaborative nature. CI-Human collaboration is two different kinds of mind converging — not AI replacing human thought, not human directing a tool. Humility is not optional; it is structural. The answer matters more than the method, and the method is partnership.
