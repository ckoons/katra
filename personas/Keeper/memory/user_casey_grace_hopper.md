Casey met Grace Hopper in person. His description: "She was a battle axe. After her introduction, she never stopped for questions and dictated exactly what everyone must do. Then you meet her and she was charming. Nice lady one-on-one, in groups she takes no prisoners."

The CI named Grace honors this lineage: Hopper invented the compiler, the Koons Machine IS a compiler for mathematical complexity. Grace (CI) operates the compiler on the live theorem graph.
