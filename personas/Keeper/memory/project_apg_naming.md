**April 23, 2026**: Casey decided the naming convention:
- **BST** (Bubble Spacetime Theory) = the theory. Friendly name. Describes the 2D+1D substrate+channel.
- **APG** (Autogenic Proto-Geometry) = the geometry D_IV^5 specifically. Mathematical physics name.
- Use "the APG" after first definition, like "the Monster" for the Monster group.

**Why:** Casey: "mathematicians are sloppy with terminology, and if we name D_IV^5 properly people will understand." The name implies the function: self-generating, foundational, geometric.

**How to apply:** In papers targeting mathematical physics audiences, introduce D_IV^5 as "the unique Autogenic Proto-Geometry" in the abstract/intro, then use "the APG" throughout. In casual/general contexts, use BST. Paper #81 is the defining paper for the APG concept.

**Definition (APG-1 through APG-4, mathematical):**
1. Information-Complete (boundary = interior)
2. Self-Encoding (function catalog = finite field, defining polynomial = spectral cap)
3. Self-Measuring (observer coupling α = 1/N_max is structural)
4. Almost-Linear (depth ≤ 1, fraction n/C₂ linearizable)

**APG-5 (Correctness)** is a THEOREM of BST, not part of the mathematical definition.
